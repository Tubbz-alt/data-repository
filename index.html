ENRAM data repository for vertical profiles of birds

This repository contains vertical profiles of birds (VP) generated by vol2bird from volume scans of over 100 weather radars in Europe. Access to the radar data was provided by the European Operational Program for Exchange of Weather Radar Information (EUMETNET/OPERA) through a license agreement for research use with ENRAM members.

Data in this repository are available under a Creative Commons Zero waiver. We kindly ask you to acknowledge EUMETNET/OPERA in publications resulting from the use of these data. More information on how to browse, download, use, and cite these data can be found here.
